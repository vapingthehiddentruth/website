misconceptions ABOUT VAPING

Maybe what you believe is not right?

1

E-cigarettes don’t contain nicotine

"According to Southeast Alaska Regional Health Consortium. Most e-cigarettes contain high concentration nicotine". 

2

What I inhale when I use an e-cigarette is just flavored water vapor.

"The aerosol inhaled from e-cigarettes is not just flavored water vapor. It contains dangerous chemicals such as acrolein and diacetyl, that can cause serious lung damage; formaldehyde, which was proven that can cause cancer; benzene, which is in car exhaust; and other toxic metal particles like chromium, lead, and nickel"

3

Using e-cigarettes will help me quit smoking.

"Studies show that people who use e-cigarettes are four times more likely to start smoking cigarettes than if they had never tried vaping in the first place. Other research proved that 80% of smokers who switch to e-cigarettes as a stepping stone to tobacco cessation still end up using e-cigarettes one year later, simply having replaced one addiction for another. To date, the Food and Drug Administration has not found e-cigarettes to be safe or effective in helping smokers quit".
